MyPhotoBooth

Turn your Android phone into a simple portable photo booth for events, parties, family photos, and more.

About MyPhotoBooth

MyPhotoBooth is an Android app that lets you use your phone like a photo booth. Set up your device, choose your camera, start a timed photo session, and let the app capture photos automatically.

Features

  • Front and back camera support
  • Timed interval photo capture
  • Photo sessions based on time or photo count
  • Photos saved directly to your device
  • Simple setup for parties, events, and family use

Privacy Policy

MyPhotoBooth uses your device camera to take photos during photo booth sessions. Photos are saved on your device. The app does not sell your personal photos.

MyPhotoBooth may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may use device and advertising data to show ads and measure ad performance.
